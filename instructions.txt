1. Download the zip file and unzip it.
2. The Main.ipynb implements the model. 
3. The Midi_To_Img.ipynb converts midi files to corrosponding piano rolls and saves as a png image.
4. The IMG_To_Midi.ipynb converts a png image, into corrosponding midi.
5. The load_model.ipynb loads the pre trained model and generated new distributions (png images).
6. All lines in all files are self explainatory, if you still have a doubt then feel free to mail me.
